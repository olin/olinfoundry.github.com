History of the Foundry

Olin was founded in 2001 is the mission to resdesign engineering education and beocme a model for this new type of education that merges project-based engineering with entrenpurship and liberal arts to build engineers who can think broadly about the world problems. Olin inhrerently is not just a school -- it is in itself a startup, celebrating its 10 year anniversary in 2011.

The Foundry, Olin's entreprnuship club and incubator, sprung up during the first years of Olin as a place where students could come work on projects. Since then, it has morphed into a club and incubator and has relocated from our beloved space at Edison House to three rooms in the campus center.

The Foundry Board aims to encourage entrepnruship on campus through running events such as Tech Talks and the Olin Startup Fair, giving students money to explore business ideas, and helping students get to entreprenurial events in Boston.

If you are interested in coming to give a Tech Talk, registering for the Olin Startup Fair, or just learning more about what the Foundry is doing, please feel free to contact us .
