Foundry Space for Spring 2013

CC326 is an open space for students to work on their projects. Please feel free to utilize the space in the large room. If you would like storage space for your stuff, please operate under Olin's Honor Code and "Don't Be That Guy," but feel free to leave stuff up on the walls.

If you finished the coffee in the espresso maker, please refill it or let the board know if we are out.
